How to Resize Avatar Items in Roblox


Within the popular online game Roblox, there exists a feature that was newly added that allows the player to resize avatar items, which allows so much more player customization and immersion. Despite being one of the most promising avatar features after the addition of player made character models and animated heads, this feature is still very, very new. This means that not many people know that it exists, let alone know how to use it. So, this will be a helpful tutorial on how to resize avatar accesories in Roblox.
